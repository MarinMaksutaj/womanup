Woman Up!

About Us

WomenUp! focuses on bringing women entrepreneurs and local business owners with potential customers. The main purpose of WomanUp! is to provide a platform for these women to offer services to people. Entrepreneurs will be able to create their profiles on WomanUp! and will also have the option to edit their information later. The customers can search for women entrepreneurs by the name of the service, name of the category, or by description. The customers will be able to contact business owners through the built-in chat feature.
